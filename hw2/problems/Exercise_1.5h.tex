A DFA must be constructed such that it accepts the language \setsuch{w}{w \neq \text{``a''} \text{ and } w \neq \text{``b''}}.
\par
Let $M$ be the DFA that accepts the language \setsuch{w}{w = \text{``a''} \text{ or } w = \text{``b''}}:
\begin{figure}[H] \centering \caption*{$M$} \begin{tikzpicture}
	\GraphInit[vstyle=Dijkstra]
	\tikzset{LabelStyle/.style = {fill=white}}
	
	\Vertex[Math, x=0, y=2]{q_0}
	\Vertex[Math, x=2, y=2]{q_1}
	\Vertex[Math, x=0, y=0]{q_2}
	\Vertex[Math, x=2, y=0]{q_3}
	
	\Edge[label=a,style={->, bend left}](q_0)(q_1)
	\Edge[label=b,style={->, bend right}](q_0)(q_2)
	\Edge[label={a,b},style={->, bend left}](q_1)(q_3)
	\Edge[label={a,b},style={->, bend right}](q_2)(q_3)
	
	\Loop[label={a,b},dist=1cm,dir=SOEA,style{->}](q_3)
	
	\draw (2,2) circle (0.25cm);
	\draw (0,0) circle (0.25cm);
	\draw[thick,->] (0,2.75) -- (0,2.3);
	
\end{tikzpicture} \end{figure}
