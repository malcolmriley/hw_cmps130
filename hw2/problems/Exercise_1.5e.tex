A DFA that accepts the language \setsuch{w}{w \notin \text{(ab*)*}} must be constructed.
\par
Let $M$ be a DFA that accepts the language (ab*)*:

	\Vertex[Math, x=4, y=0]{q_2}

	\Edge[label=b,style={->, bend left}](q_1)(q_2)
	\Edge[label=a,style={->, bend left}](q_2)(q_1)
	
	\Loop[label=b,dist=1cm,dir=SO,style{->}](q_0)
